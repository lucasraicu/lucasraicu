Glenbrook Symphonic Orchestra

I am the co-concert master of the Glenbrook Symphony Orchestra (GSO). The GSO is the Northfield, Illinois District 225 Orchestra, combining students from both Glenbrook North and Glenbrook South High Schools. The GSO has over 150 student musicians and a full complement of instruments from strings, wind, and percusion.
